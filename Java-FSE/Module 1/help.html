How to Use the Community Portal

  • Home: View the welcome message and navigate to sections easily.
  • Events: Browse upcoming events and register for one by clicking the "Register" button.
  • Find Nearby Events: Click the button to locate events close to your location (requires location access).
  • Image Gallery: View past events and double-click any image to enlarge it.
  • Register for an Event: Fill out the form with your name, email, phone number, and message to register.
  • Promo Video: Watch the promo video for an overview of community activities.
